Dr. Narinthorn Surasinthon Medical Director, VitalLife Scientific Wellness Clinic at RAKxa Bang Krachao
Specialist in Anti-aging and Regenerative Medicine

"With the right mindset, you can improve both your health and your life."

BioGraphy

Calm and compassionate, Dr. Narinthorn Surasinthon is an anti-aging and regenerative medicine specialist with particular expertise in medical wellness and preventive healthcare. He is a keen advocate of using the latest technology to detect and treat underlying health issues. Dr. Narinthorn also conducts health promotion on social media channels, where he is known by fans as Dr. Care Bear based on his acclaimed book Healthy with Dr. Care Bear. Dr. Narinthorn graduated from Thailand’s leading medical school, Mahidol University, and served as head of a wellness center before joining VitalLife.
